# Create an email campaign

This guide walks you through creating an email campaign in Subflow from start to finish. You will design the email in the visual editor, choose who receives it, set the subject line, and send the campaign immediately or schedule it for later.

## Before you begin

Here is everything you need to have ready before creating the campaign:

- A segment containing the contacts you want to reach, or the individual contacts already in your contact list. If you still need to create a segment, see [Create a Segment](#).
- Any images, logos, or links you plan to include in the email.
- An agreed subject line and an internal campaign name.
- The text content for the email body, or a clear idea of what it should say.
- A decision on when to send: immediately after you confirm, or on a scheduled date and time.

## Get started

The steps below walk you through the full process of creating and sending an email campaign.

1. Open **Messaging** from the main navigation.

2. Select **E-mail Campaigns** from the side menu.

3. Click **New Campaign** in the upper right corner. The template gallery opens.

4. Select **Start from scratch** to design the email yourself, or click any template card to open it as a starting point.

5. Enter a name in the **Name** field at the top of the editor. This name identifies the email design within the campaign.

6. Use the drag-and-drop editor to build the content of the email. The right-hand panel updates to show formatting options for whichever element you have selected.

   - Drag content blocks (headings, text, images, buttons) into the canvas from the editor controls.
   - Click any block to edit its content directly. The right-hand panel shows formatting options for that block, such as heading type (H1–H4), font family, font weight, and font size.
   - Switch between desktop and mobile views using the screen icons at the top of the canvas to check how the email will look on each device.
   - Click the eye icon to preview the finished email at any point.

7. Click **Save** in the bottom toolbar when you are done. The **Unsaved Changes** indicator shows when there is work that has not been saved yet.

8. Open the **Recipients** tab. The editor opens with three tabs at the top — **Design**, **Recipients**, and **Settings** — so you can move between them at any point.

9. To send to a group, click **Add segments** and select one or more segments from the list.

10. To send to specific people, click **Add contacts** and select individual contact records.

    > **Tip:** You can combine segments and individual contacts in the same campaign. Segments include everyone who currently meets the segment criteria, so the recipient count is calculated at send time, not when you add the segment.

11. Click **Next Step** to move to **Settings**, or click the **Settings** tab directly.

12. In the **Name** field, enter an internal name for the campaign. This is what you will see in the campaigns list, not what the recipient sees.

13. In the **Subject** field, enter the subject line that will appear in the recipient's inbox.

14. Open the **Send Now / Schedule** selector and choose when to send the campaign:

    - **Send Now:** sends the email as soon as you confirm.
    - **Schedule:** lets you pick a date and time for the email to be sent automatically.

15. Return to the **Design** tab if you want to make a last adjustment, or click the eye icon to preview the email one more time.

16. Click **Send** and confirm the action when prompted. The campaign is sent immediately, or queued to go out at the scheduled time.

    > **Note:** Once a campaign has been sent, the content can no longer be edited. A scheduled campaign can be canceled before it is sent.

17. After sending, the campaign appears in the campaigns list. The columns in the list show the following information:

    - **Subject:** the subject line you entered for the campaign.
    - **Status:** whether the campaign is Sent or Scheduled.
    - **Recipients:** how many contacts the email was delivered to.
    - **Delivered, Open Rate, Clicked, and Bounced:** how the email performed once it was sent.
    - **Updated Date:** the most recent action taken on the campaign.

    Click any campaign in the list to see exactly what was sent, or, for a scheduled campaign, what is queued to go out.
